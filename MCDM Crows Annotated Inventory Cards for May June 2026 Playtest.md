# MCDM Crows Annotated Inventory Cards for May/June 2026 Playtest

This document is a faithful transcription of the playtest PDF's annotated inventory cards. Each card is reproduced with its printed fields. PDF "sticky-note" / highlight annotations are reproduced verbatim under an **Annotations** subsection beneath the specific card they are attached to.

The cards in the source PDF are laid out in a 5-column grid per page. The transcription below preserves that grid order, walking left-to-right and top-to-bottom on each page.
