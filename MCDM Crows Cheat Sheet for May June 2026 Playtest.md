# CROWS Cheatsheet for May June 2026 Playtest

## Tests

- 2d10 + Characteristic Score (+ Skill)
- Bonuses and penalties can apply per Ref
- Natural 2 or 3 is a doom
- 11 or less failure, bad, or least good
- 12-16 is partial progress or mixed result
- 17+ is good
- Natural 19-20 is a crit

## Damage

- Reduces Stamina (AD first if wearing armor)
- When Stamina is 0 you start taking wounds
- Wounds fill backpack slots
- When all backpack slots full of wounds = dead

## Dungeon Turn (DT)

- Each is 30 minutes or real time.
- Roll DT usage dice at the end.
- Encounter check at the end.

## Resting

- 6 hours long, must spend 4 sleeping
- Encounter check every 2 hours
- Must eat ration and have bed(roll)
- Regain all Stamina
- Remove 1 wound
- Can perform 1 rest activity

## Usage Dice

- Always d6s
- Roll when item is used or end of DT
- If 1 or 2 comes up, remove that usage die
- All usage dice gone? Item/effect stops
- Spellbooks regain all usage dice on rest

## Counter

- If missed with a melee attack, you can counter
- Counter is 12-16 result of melee weapon in hand

## Light

- **Dim Light:** Hard to see. -2 penalty on tests.
- **Darkness:** Can't see. -4 penalty on tests.

## Inventory

- 2 hand slots, 2 belt slots, and 10 backpack slots.
- Like items (daggers, wands) can be stacked.
- Hand items are equipped.
- Armor takes up backpack slots when worn.
- Belt items can be drawn as a maneuver.
- Backpack items drawn as a maneuver. Roll 1d10. If result meets or beats the slot, you draw the item. If lower, nothing drawn, can rearrange.

## Combat Actions and Maneuvers

- 1 action, 1 maneuver, 1 reaction
- **Common Maneuver:** Move speed, knockback, grab, escape grab, shift, draw/pick up item, stand up
- **Triggered:** Opportunity attack/counter
- **Crit on Attack:** Extra action
- **Flanking:** +1 on melee attack roll

### Grab: 2d10 + S + Unarmed skill

- *≤11:* The target can counter.
- *12-16:* The target is grabbed by you and can counter you.
- *17+:* The target is grabbed by you.

### Escape Grab: 2d10 + A or S + Unarmed skill

- *≤11:* The grabber can counter.
- *12-16:* You are no longer grabbed, and the grabber can counter.
- *17+:* You are no longer grabbed.

### Knockback: 2d10 + S + Unarmed skill (Melee 1)

- *≤11:* The target can counter.
- *12-16:* push 1
- *17+:* push 2

## Conditions

- **Grabbed:** Speed 0, +1 on attacks against you
- **Prone:** -1 on melee attacks, +1 on melee attacks against you, -1 ranged against you, speed halved
- **Boned:** -1 on all tests and +1 damage dealt to you on incoming damage equal to boned level. Disappears at DT end.
- **Blessed:** +1 on all tests and +1 damage you deal equal to blessed level. Disappears at DT end.
- **Boned and Blessed:** Cancel out effects 1 to 1.

## Doom

- Doom on casting spell = Backlash
- Doom on melee = 17+ counter dam is 17+ result
- Doom on ranged = 17+ dam dealt to adjacent ally

## Ranged into Melee

- Attack a target adjacent to ally and miss, hit ally instead for 12-16 result.
- Attack adjacent target with ranged attack, -1.

## Critical Hit

- Critical hit with attack gives extra action
- Critical hit with spell no usage die roll for book

## Armor

If you wear armor, it gives you AD. When you take damage, it comes from AD first. When AD hits 0, your armor is broken. AD can be restored by repairing armor as a rest activity.

## Chaos Count

When a PC gets a 11 or lower result that isn't doom while casting a spell, the Ref adds 1d6 + the spell's rank to the chaos count. When the Ref has 13 chaos, the triggering spell has a backlash.
